    FAQ

    • What can I frame?

      If you love it, you can frame it! Simple posters and prints, valuable works of art on paper or canvas, cherished documents, favorite photos, family heirlooms, 3D objects, mirrors.

    • Will you help find the perfect frame for my art?

      Unsure of what your item needs? No problem! Our team will guide you through the process of finding theframe, style, and proportions for your art and for your home–for a frame you’ll love for a lifetime.

    • What type of frames do you offer?

      Classic, modern, ornate, traditional, or rustic–we have the perfect frame for you.

    • Are your picture frames shipped pre-assembled?

      Frames purchased with components (mat board, mount board, and/or glazing) are normally shipped fully assembled. Wood picture frames, which are permanently hand-glued and nailed together, are always shipped assembled. Metal picture frames are often shipped assembled, but may be shipped unassembled for greater protection, or if they are ordered without contents. If you have questions about picture frame assembly, leave a note in the Comments box of the checkout page or contact us before you place your order.

    • Do you offer Express Shipping?

      Yes, orders with express shipping (Express Saver, 2nd Day, or Standard Overnight) selected during checkout are given high priority. Please note that the transit time for express shipping excludes weekends.
